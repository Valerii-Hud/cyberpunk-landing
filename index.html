Night-City will change you forever!
Cyberpunk 2077 - is an adventure role-playing game, the action of which takes place in the metropolis of Night City, where power, luxury and body modifications are valued above all. You play as V, a mercenary in search of a device to gain immortality. You can change your character's cyber implants, skills, and play style while exploring an open world where your actions affect the course of the story and everything that surrounds you.
